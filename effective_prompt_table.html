Element What it means Example
Context Background information the AI needs to understand the situation. This includes who you are, what the data represents and any relevant constraints. "I am a finance manager preparing monthly management accounts for a mid-sized retail company with four stores."
Task A clear, specific description of what you want the AI to do. The more precise, the better. "Please reconcile these two datasets by matching transactions on date, amount and description."
Format How you want the output structured. Without this, the AI will choose its own structure, which may not match what you need. "Present the results as a summary table with columns for matched, likely matched, and unmatched items, followed by a detailed list of unmatched transactions."
Constraints Boundaries, rules, tone, length or quality standards the output must meet. These prevent common problems before they occur. "Flag any item where the variance exceeds $5,000 or 10%. Do not infer explanations for variances. List them as requiring investigation."
