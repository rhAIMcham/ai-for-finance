Performance Headline
Group revenue of $9,755k beat budget by $355k (+3.8%) and exceeded the prior period by $575k (+6.3%). NPAT of $770k was $175k above budget (+29.4%). EBITDA of $2,207k was $143k below budget (−6.1%), partly offset by a $160k approved marketing deferral and $132k salary savings from two Technology BU vacancies.
Material Movements
Revenue & EBITDA
Revenue beat reflects three Technology BU enterprise contracts, two pulled forward from Q3 (confirmed by Technology GM). Gross margin held at 66.5%. EBITDA missed budget on Legal & Compliance costs of $500k vs budget $298k (+$202k, +67.8%) — driver unconfirmed; GC to report by 15 Jul. Marketing deferral ($160k) and salary savings ($132k) partially offset the miss.
EBIT, NPAT & Tax
EBIT of $1,285k exceeded budget by $260k (+25.4%); D&A stable at $922k. NPAT of $770k beat budget by $175k (+29.4%). Tax charge of $330k was $75k above budget (+29.4%) — higher Technology BU taxable income at statutory rate; no adjusting items.
Cash & Working Capital
Cash of $5,320k was $520k above budget (+10.8%). Revolving credit drawn $800k within the board-approved $2,000k limit. Net working capital of $2,895k is above the $2,500k policy minimum. DSO extended to 52 days (+7 days) — full movement requires confirmation. Net debt $1,320k within covenant thresholds.
Risks & Watch Items
Q3 Revenue Pipeline
Two Q2 enterprise deals were pulled forward from Q3, reducing pipeline coverage. Q3 revenue target is at risk. Board to approve revised Q3 forecast at 28 Jul meeting.
Operations BU Margin
EBITDA margin fell to 8.9% in Q2 from 18.4% in Q1 on a $490k revenue decline with fixed headcount. Recovery options to be presented at August ExCo.
Legal & Compliance Run-Rate
Q2 costs of $500k compare to $278k in Q1. If Q3 recurs at this rate, FY2025 legal costs will exceed budget by ~$450k. Driver unconfirmed. GC explanation due 15 Jul.
Doubtful Debt
Provision increased $45k in Q2; one client in administration. No write-off taken. Receivables team monitoring.
Decisions Required from the Board
1
Approve the revised Q3 revenue forecast (Technology BU pipeline pull-forward). To be tabled 28 July.
2
Receive GC's explanation of Legal & Compliance variance ($202k, +67.8%) and approve any further engagement if costs are expected to continue at Q2 levels.

Approved Communication — Q2 FY2025

Acme Corporation is pleased to report the following results for Q2 FY2025, for the period ended 30 June 2025.

Acme Corporation delivered group revenue of $9.8 million for Q2 FY2025, ahead of budget and up 6.3% on the prior period, reflecting strong enterprise demand in our Technology division.

Group EBITDA of $2.2 million represents a 22.6% margin, an improvement of 2.9 percentage points on the prior period.

The Group maintained a strong cash position of $5.3 million at 30 June 2025, with net working capital above policy thresholds.

The Company does not provide forward earnings guidance. Management continues to monitor pipeline conversion and cost trends.

This communication has been prepared in accordance with information available as at the date of issue and reflects results for Q2 FY2025 only.
